PING-PONG
The next 'big' thing.

This is a very simple game, but its not actual ping-pong per se.
These are the guidelines:

  1. You enter a number in the space provided
  2. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  3. Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  4. Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"
